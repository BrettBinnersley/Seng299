Welcome to the Fernwood Farmers' Market

The Fernwood Farmers' Market is available each and every day all year round, because holidays are for lesser people who lack our mettle for raw business. Come to our market to find fresh meals at lunch time for those of you too lazy to pack your own lunch. Vegans, hippies, and hipsters may enjoy our large selections of fresh produce straight from the fields, maggots and worms included. The economical ones amoung you can find cheap trinkets and and other homemade presents of assured low quality which are ideal for birthdays and christmas, you cheap bastard.

We are located in Fernwood Square in the heart of the thriving metropolis that is Victoria. Guarenteed parking will be available for registered vendors, all other parking will be first come first serve and will be highly expensive with awkward payment intervals and parking attendants who will watch your time remaining the the ferocity and exactness of the scientists at CERN. Those of you too lazy or stupid to look up where Fernwood Square is in order to find us can refer to the helpful map below and to the right. Consider this a barrier for entrance and those of you who who are still unable to locate the market can consider your business unwanted.

The more entrepreneurial amoung you may be wondering how you can get in on this market and begin hawking your garbage and compostable scraps for great returns. The process is simple, first register for an account on this site and specify your account type as 'vendor'. Next locate a market staff member and beg, bribe, threaten, blackmail, or perform interpretive dance for in order to get them to approve your vendor application which will allow you to begin booking your booths.
